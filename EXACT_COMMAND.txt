## QUICK FIX FOR POWERShell PATH ISSUE:

The problem is PowerShell interprets "C+" as a parameter. You MUST use quotes:
- ❌ WRONG: `cd C:\Users\Administrator\Desktop\CLI C+ Root\rootserver`
- ✅ CORRECT: `cd "C:\Users\Administrator\Desktop\CLI C+ Root\rootserver"`

Or use the 8.3 short name:
```powershell
cd C:\Users\ADMINI~1\Desktop\CLIC+~1\rootserver
